Business Requirements Document

Objective: Build a centralized sales management system to organize workflows, prevent lead leakage, and structure client data accurately based on the unique B2B conformity assessment model.

01 Foundational Data Model & Identity Management

Core Architecture Principle: The system is built on a "Conformed Data Model" where the Commercial Registration (CR) Number acts as the absolute unique identifier (Primary Key equivalent) for all Facilities. This ensures zero duplication and creates a single source of truth across all modules.

🗄️ 1.1 Structural Entity Relationships & Core Logic

Core Entity

🏭 Facility

The actual manufacturer or plant receiving the certificate. Inherits from "Company" table.

🚨 Logic: CR Number MUST be unique. Auto-generates upon Lead "Qualified" stage.
⚡ UI Requirement (Quick Action): Must include a persistent "Quick Action Button" for instant Support Tickets / Activities without leaving the profile.
Payer Entity

🤝 Client / Broker

The entity financially responsible for the request. They are stored in the SAME database table, differentiated only by a toggle.

⚙️ The Boolean Logic (Is Broker?):
If OFF (Direct Client)
The Facility Itself
If ON (Broker)
3rd Party Agency

Note on Renewals: Renewal deals skip the Lead stage entirely and auto-clone the existing Facility + Client/Broker linkage.

Intake Entity

🎯 Lead

Initial, transient prospect record from external sources.

⚙️ Backend Logic (Split & Commit): Upon "Qualified", system splits data into 3 tables:
Deal + Facility + Broker

💰 Deal (The Dual-Link Rule)

A Deal MUST be linked to exactly ONE Facility and ONE Client/Broker simultaneously. Multiple Deals can exist for the exact same CR Number at the same time (e.g., Renewal + New cert).

📝 Activity & Logs

Immutable records of interactions. System Logic: Must strictly belong to a parent record (Deal, Lead, Ticket, or Facility) to ensure traceability. Cannot exist standalone.

📋 5.2 Task SLAs & Centralized Board

The "Deep Dive" Execution (UX Requirement)

Activities MUST NOT be siloed. Reps open ONE Centralized "My Activities" board to execute all daily tasks across all modules. To prevent redundant clicks and ensure full context, execution MUST follow this strict UI flow:

1 The Card & Tag

Every task card MUST display a dynamic Source Tag (e.g., [Lead], [Deal]).

2 The Redirect

Clicking the card DOES NOT open a pop-up. It instantly redirects the user inside the actual parent record.

3 Action Widget

Inside the profile, a prominent widget appears above the timeline (e.g., "⚠️ Planned Call Today").

4 The Loop & Save

User clicks "Mark Done", logs mandatory notes, schedules "Next Follow-up", and original card disappears.

⏰
Overdue Task SLA Escalation

If a scheduled task/activity passes its deadline without being marked "Done" or rescheduled, an automated escalation email is immediately sent to the Sales Rep and CRM Manager.

10 Detailed User Story Catalog & Epics

Developer-ready agile user stories formulated in strict As a... I want to... So that... architecture.

  • US 1.1: As an Admin, I want to create user accounts and assign dynamic roles (Permission Groups) so that I can easily merge or separate department accesses.
  • US 1.2: As a User, I want a secure login with an automated "Forgot Password" link so that I can manage my access without IT intervention.
  • US 1.3: As a Manager, I want to toggle a Sales Rep’s status to "On Leave", and the system MUST prompt me to select a temporary delegate, so that the absent rep's active deals are auto-routed without delay.
  • US 2.1: As a User, I want to create a Lead with mandatory fields (Name, Phone, Service, Source) so that we maintain high data quality from the start.
  • US 2.2: As the System, I want to automatically route leads based on Country and use Round Robin for distribution, actively skipping users marked as "On Leave", so that workload is fair.
  • US 2.3: As a Sales Rep, when I enter a "CR Number" for a new facility, I want the system to instantly check the database and show a red warning with a link if it exists, so that duplicate facilities are prevented at the entry point.
  • US 2.4: As a Manager/Admin, I want a dedicated "Merge Tool" to scan for fuzzy duplicate facilities and merge their contacts and activities into a single "Keep Old" record, so that the database remains clean.
  • US 3.1: As a Sales Rep, when creating a Quotation, I want it split into 2 Tabs: Tab 1 (Commercial) for pricing and Tab 2 (Technical) for Product Category, Section, HS Code, and TR, so that operational details are perfectly captured.
  • US 3.2: As a Sales Rep, I want the system to auto-fetch the unit price from the backend Service Catalog once I select a service and authority, minimizing manual entry errors.
  • US 3.3: As a Sales Rep, if I apply a discount, I want the quote status to automatically change to "Pending Approval" and route to my Manager.
  • US 3.4: As a Manager, if I reject a discount, I must be forced to enter a mandatory "Rejection Reason", providing clear feedback to the Rep.
  • US 3.5: As a Sales Rep, when marking a deal as "Lost", I must be forced to select a churn reason from a dropdown and write a mandatory explanatory note.
  • US 4.1: As a Sales Rep, I want to log Calls, Emails, WhatsApps, and Meetings directly on a deal’s timeline.
  • US 4.2: As a Sales Rep, when I log a "Follow-up" activity, the system MUST force me to select a "Next Activity Date" before saving, ensuring continuous engagement.
  • US 4.3: As the System, I want to track Absolute Inactivity Days. At 4 days (send alert), at 5 days (card turns orange), at 6 days (apply a red ⚠ SLA Breach badge).
  • US 4.4: As a Manager, I want to receive a critical alert if a "New Lead" has 0 logged activities within the first 24 hours of assignment.
  • US 4.5: As a User, I want a read-only "WhatsApp View" tab inside the Deal profile fetching chat history via API integration, keeping official communications centralized.
  • US 5.1: As an After-Sales Rep, I want the system to automatically trigger renewal alerts 30 days before Standard certificates expire, and 7 months before SQM certificates expire.
  • US 5.2: As a Manager, I want an automated weekly summary email reporting all pending and upcoming renewals.
  • US 5.3: As a User, I want a "One-Click Renew" button on expiring certificates that instantly creates a new Deal in the "Negotiation" stage, prepopulating all CR, Facility, and Client data.
  • US 6.1: As a Customer, I want to submit a ticket via the Website form, which auto-creates a record in the CRM Support Pipeline.
  • US 6.2: As a Sales/Ops User, I want to open an internal ticket directly from a Deal or Facility profile, auto-linking it to the CR Number.
  • US 6.3: As the System, I want tickets to follow a strict separate pipeline (New ➔ In Progress ➔ Waiting ➔ Resolved / Closed).
  • US 6.4: As a Manager, I want SLA timers specifically for Tickets (Response Time & Resolution Time) to monitor customer satisfaction.
  • US 7.1: As a Manager, I want a dynamic dashboard showing Team Pipeline Value, Win Rate, Neglected Deals, and CLV ranking.
  • US 7.2: As a Sales Rep, I want a personal KPI dashboard tracking my Calls/Day, Tasks Due, Qualified Rate, and Monthly Revenue.

Email Connection

Phase 2.2

WhatsApp View (Internal Tab)

🚫 Critical Business Rule:

To avoid database clutter, incoming WhatsApp messages WILL NOT auto-create leads or sync randomly to the pipeline.

Instead, a specific "WhatsApp View" Tab will be embedded inside the Deal/Lead profile. Users can open this tab to read the synced conversation history (Read-only iframe/API) strictly tied to that client's phone number.
