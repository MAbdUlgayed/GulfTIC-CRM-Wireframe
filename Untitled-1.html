Executive Summary & Objectives

Centralizing operations to eliminate lead leakage and establish automated, accountable sales execution.

⚠️ Current Operational Problems

  • ▪️ Data Leakage: Leads scattered across Excel and WhatsApp.
  • ▪️ No Accountability: No clear assignment logic or ownership clarity.
  • ▪️ Poor Discipline: Inconsistent sales follow-up and lack of SLA enforcement.
  • ▪️ Data Integrity: Severe duplicate client issues due to no standard DB.
  • ▪️ Manual BI: Reporting is manually prepared and inherently inaccurate.

🎯 Primary Business Goals

  • ▪️ Centralization: Unify all leads and customers in a single, secure system.
  • ▪️ Conversion Uplift: Increase conversion rates & reduce lead response times.
  • ▪️ Enforce SLAs: Establish automated sales accountability & prevent lead neglect.
  • ▪️ Live Analytics: Enable real-time management KPI visibility via dashboards.
  • ▪️ Scalability: Build a pristine data foundation for Phase 2 AI automation.

SYSTEM Assigned Rep
AUTOMATED SYSTEM RULES

Duplicate Check: Triggers on Mobile, Email, or Company Name.
Auto-Routing: Geographic mapping (e.g., UAE leads ➔ UAE team).

Facility Entity (Importer/Exporter)

The actual physical factory or company that the legal certificate will be issued to.

FIELD Company Name
FIELD CR Number
FIELD Country & Address
FIELD Industry
FIELD Registration Data
CORE RELATIONSHIP CONSTRAINT

Facilities do NOT directly pay invoices or act as communicators in many scenarios; they must be strictly linked to 'Clients' or 'Brokers'.

Client Entity (Communicator/Payer)

The person or agency dealing with GulfTIC to process the job and pay invoices.

Internal Employee

This contact works directly inside the associated Facility. Communications and invoices are handled directly with the manufacturer.

Standard Fields

Name, Phone, Email, Role, Linked Facilities Map.

Opportunity Entity (Deal)

The transactional revenue record tracking the certification lifecycle.

RELATIONAL Facility & Client Map
FINANCIAL Service Type & Value
PIPELINE Expected Close Date
OWNERSHIP Stage & Owner Rep
TRACKING Lost Reason (Mandatory), Next Action Date, Audit Logs

❗ Critical Rule: "WON" Stage

"Won" does NOT mean the certificate is issued. That is an operations metric.

Deal triggers Won when:
1. Client officially confirms order.
2. Required docs are submitted.
3. Operation request initiated.

Deep Dive Document

1. Strategic Overview & Stakeholders

Project Vision

Phase 1 focuses on building a centralized, immutable sales management framework tailored to GulfTIC's unique B2B business model. By dismantling data silos (Excel, WhatsApp) and implementing strict structural relationships (Facilities vs. Clients), the system will prevent lead leakage and lay the foundation for advanced future automation.

Stakeholder Hierarchy & Functional Requirements

💼

Sales Representative

Pipeline Execution

Primary Goal: Conversion of assigned leads efficiently.

Required Tools: Visual Kanban boards, quick-action activity logging, automated follow-up reminders.

Data Scope: Siloed view. Only sees owned records.

📈

Sales Manager

Strategic Oversight

Primary Goal: Team oversight and workload balancing.

Required Tools: Reporting dashboards, lead reassignment utility, SLA breach monitoring alerts.

Data Scope: Hierarchical view of the entire branch team.

♻️

After-Sales Specialist

Client Retention

Primary Goal: Proactive renewal management.

Required Tools: Automated expiry date trackers (30d/7m), one-click renewal opportunity generator.

🎯

Marketing Exec

Lead Generation

Primary Goal: High-quality lead generation and ROI tracking.

Required Tools: Lead source tracking, website API integration logs, campaign conversion analytics.

💰

Finance Department

Revenue Control

Primary Goal: Accurate revenue forecasting and quotation standardization.

Required Tools: Scheme-specific pricing rules matrices, discount approval workflow engine.

3. Detailed Module Specifications

M1 Lead Capture & Distribution

Web/Email API Integration

The CRM exposes a secure REST API endpoint to receive payloads directly from corporate landing pages. This bypasses manual data entry completely.

POST /api/v1/leads/intake
{
  "source": "website_contact_form",
  "contact_name": "Ahmed Manufacturer",
  "mobile": "+971501234567",
  "country": "UAE",
  "service_interest": "ECAS Electrical"
}

Leave-Aware Round Robin Engine

Algorithm logic for distributing centrally generated leads to ensure fair workload and immediate prospect response.

  • Query active Sales Reps within the Lead's Target Country group.
  • Check `Leave_Status` toggle. If TRUE, skip user.
  • Assign to the valid user with the oldest `Last_Assigned_Timestamp`.

M2 After-Sales Renewal Engine

CRON JOB

Dynamic Escalation Tracker

System runs a daily check against the `Certificate_Expiry_Date` field.

Rule A: Standard Certificate ➔ Alert at T-30 Days.
Rule B: SQM Certificate ➔ Alert at T-7 Months.

One-Click Conversion Workflow

UI must provide a 'RENEW' button on expiring records. Clicking this executes the following transaction:

  1. Generate new Deal record in Pipeline.
  2. Clone Facility mapping.
  3. Clone Client/Broker mapping.
  4. Tag source as "Auto-Renewal".

Immutable Audit Logging

Every database transaction (Create, Edit, State Change) automatically writes to an append-only Audit Log. Essential for compliance and dispute resolution.
