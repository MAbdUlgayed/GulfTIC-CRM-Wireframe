Executive Summary & Objectives

Centralizing operations to eliminate lead leakage and establish automated, accountable sales execution.

⚠️ Current Operational Problems

  • ▪️ Leads scattered across Excel and WhatsApp (Data Leakage)
  • ▪️ No clear assignment logic or accountability
  • ▪️ Inconsistent sales follow-up discipline
  • ▪️ Severe duplicate client issues (No standard DB)
  • ▪️ Poor retention & renewal process for certificates
  • ▪️ Reporting is manually prepared and inaccurate

🎯 Primary Business Goals

  • ▪️ Centralize all leads and customers in a single system
  • ▪️ Increase conversion rate & reduce response times
  • ▪️ Enforce sales accountability & prevent lead neglect
  • ▪️ Enable real-time management KPI visibility
  • ▪️ Build scalable data foundation for phase 2 automation

Core Data Architecture

Interactive breakdown of the foundational data models and unique business logic (Facilities vs. Clients).

Lead Entity

A potential inquiry before full qualification. Raw data entry point.

Field Name & Mobile
Field Email & Country
Field Source & Industry
System Assigned Rep
System Status
System Rules Duplicate check on: Mobile, Email, Company Name (Shows ⚠ warning). Auto-assign: UAE->UAE team, Egypt->Egypt team.

Facility Entity (Importer/Exporter)

The actual factory or company that the legal certificate will be issued to.

Field Company Name
Field CR Number
Field Country & Address
Field Industry
Field Registration Data
Core Relationship Facilities do not directly pay invoices or communicate; they are linked to 'Clients'.

Client Entity (Communicator/Payer)

The person or company we are dealing with to get the job done and pay invoices. Linked to one or more Facilities.

Internal Employee

This contact works directly inside the associated Facility. Communications are direct.

Standard Fields Name, Phone, Email, Linked Facilities

Opportunity Entity (Deal)

The actual revenue opportunity tracking the sales process.

Relational Facility & Client/Broker
Financial Service Type & Value
Pipeline Expected Close Date
Pipeline Stage & Owner
Tracking Lost Reason, Next Action Date, Activity Logs

❗ Critical Business Rule: "WON"

Won does NOT mean the certificate is issued (handled by Operations).

Deal becomes Won when:
1. Client officially confirms order OR
2. Required docs submitted OR
3. Operation request initiated internally.
